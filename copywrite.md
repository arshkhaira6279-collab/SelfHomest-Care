## 3. Services Section

At Safe Homenest Care, we provide person-centred NDIS support services designed to promote independence, dignity, and meaningful community participation.

### Assistance with Daily Living (ADLs)

We support participants with everyday personal care and routine activities to maintain comfort, safety, and independence. This may include:

- Showering, grooming and dressing
- Toileting and hygiene support
- Meal preparation and feeding assistance
- Medication reminders
- Mobility and transfers

Our team ensures respectful, private, and culturally sensitive care at all times.

### Community Participation

We help participants stay connected and engaged within their community. Support may include:

- Attending social events and cultural programs
- Going to recreational activities
- Visiting friends and family
- Joining clubs, classes, or volunteering

Our goal is to reduce isolation and build confidence in social settings.

### Travel & Transportation Support

We provide safe and reliable assistance with travel so participants can attend:

- Medical appointments
- Therapy sessions
- Community activities
- Shopping trips
- Work or education programs

We ensure safe transfers and comfortable transport arrangements.

### Domestic Assistance

We support participants to maintain a clean, safe, and healthy home environment. Services may include:

- Light cleaning
- Laundry
- Dishwashing
- Changing bed linens
- Basic household organisation

We encourage participants to be involved where possible to build independence.

### Life Skills Development

We help participants build practical skills to increase independence and confidence. This may include:

- Budgeting and money management
- Cooking and meal planning
- Personal organisation
- Communication skills
- Decision-making skills

We focus on capacity building tailored to each participant's goals.

### Activity Participation

We support involvement in structured and meaningful activities that promote wellbeing, such as:

- Creative programs
- Fitness and wellbeing activities
- Cultural programs
- Skill-based workshops

These activities enhance confidence, independence, and social connection.

### Support Coordination

We assist participants in understanding and implementing their NDIS plans by:

- Connecting them with suitable service providers
- Coordinating supports
- Monitoring progress toward goals
- Ensuring services meet their needs

We work closely with participants and families to make the process simple and stress-free.
